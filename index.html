Our Story

DoskiWaAfrika is a pioneering electronic music artist who fuses traditional African rhythms with contemporary house music production. Hailing from Koster in South Africa's North-West province, his sound is deeply rooted in African musical heritage while embracing modern electronic production techniques. Signed to NLM Music Group Africa, a label founded in 2025 by Ailwei Maduna Wa Malada in honor of his mother, Ntampose Lettia Maduna. Together, we're pushing the boundaries of African electronic music and bringing its unique sound to the global stage.
